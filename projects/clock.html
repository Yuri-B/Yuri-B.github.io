Re-inventing the clock

2009 - 2010

Personal project. It came from a random idea of looking at the time while thinking about spinning planets.

Process

The design process started with sketches to explore various forms and determine the easiest way to build the object.

I have disassembled a clock mechanism and built a mock-up from cardboard.

Outcome

Patent lawyers are interested in this project because of its uniqueness. Working on a patent application.

This design is not in production.
