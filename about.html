About Yuri Brigadir

In spite of experiencing break up of Soviet Union, conflict between two rival streets in Dundalk, working in start-ups for almost seven years and moving to a different continent, I'm still in one piece.

I bring creativity, systems thinking and absurdist humour. I can switch between deep theoretical and practical stuff.

My professional skills are Minimum Viable Product Development ( hardware and software ),
Design Conceptualization ( tell me to come up with visual or graphical ideas for something and I will create lots of options and sketches ),
Mechanical Design ( SolidWorks, AutoCad ),
3D Printing ( Modelling on 3D Studio MAX and finishing with hand tools ),
Website Development ( JavaScript, jQuery, HTML5, CSS3, SEO, HubSpot )

I'm learning Product Design for hardware and software ( mostly transforming business requirements into features and creating a value proposition )

Where am I now?

I'm now in Vancouver, Canada. I do snowboarding in winter, rock climbing in summer, and I get to learn something new quite often.

Timeline

  • Born in Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic

  • 1996 Saw a TV programme where a person with a stylus was tracing a sculpture and a 3D image appeared on the screen. I thought it was magical.

  • 1997 Moved to Ireland

  • 2000 - 2008 Tried a lot of stuff, mainly with computer graphics

  • 2007 - 2011 Learned industrial design and engineering
    While in Dublin Institute of Technology, I had some exposure to engineering methodology, CAD software and design critiques.

  • 2010 Nominated for Red Dot design award
    Collaborated on an experimental project to decompose cigarette butts.

  • 2011 Got mentioned in an Irish newspaper.
    Thesis project from college got mentioned in an Irish newspaper.

  • 2011 - 2016 Worked in start-ups in Dublin.
    Done a wide variety of projects, from 3D Printing to Interface Design and running a business. Had an idea for a website creator app, and then joined a start-up which created a world-class mock-up tool. Up until 2014, I thought skeumorphics were amazing.

  • 2015 Took part in improvement of democracy.
    Collaborated on development of a voting tool which was used in several public election campaigns in Ireland.

  • 2016 - 2018 Worked on retail and payment technologies in Vancouver
    Took part in building software solutions for retail and payment technologies markets. Also done some freelancing, mainly for one exciting property technology start-up.

  • Recently Explored start-up ideas
    I'm running MakerEngine on the side. I'm helping artists to own rights to sale and re-sale of their artwork.
