About Yuri Brigadir

In spite of experiencing break up of Soviet Union, conflict between Cox's and Muirhevnamor in Dundalk, working in start-ups for almost seven years and moving to a different continent, I'm still in one piece.

I can definitely: construct a select range of things with both physical and digital tools and build high performance websites with HTML / CSS / JavaScript / jQuery.

In the world of work, I call myself a prototyping specialist and generalist in several areas. My key skill is finding creative shortcuts to making things - mainly using rigorous research, optimization of resources and free thinking.

Where am I now?

I'm now in Vancouver, Canada. I do snowboarding in winter, rock climbing in summer, and I get to learn something new quite often.
